Camp Alexander

On Nepco Lake - 7 minutes away
Site of the Saturday banquet - Air Conditioned Cabins and Lodge
Three spaces with electrical hook up - Tent Camping Area
Swimming Beach - Boat Dock - Volleyball & Basketball Court
Hiking Trails - Fire Pit - 300' Zip Line - Yard Games 

We have rented Camp Alexander for the bonspiel weekend, July 13-15. There are a few things that you should know about the facility that will be helpful in making a decision about choosing this option. This is a YMCA camp that is used for a variety of camping experiences by folks of all ages, but primarily young campers. It is also rented for weddings, reunions, private parties and meetings on weekends.

While the cabins and lodge are air conditioned and comfortable, it is not a motel. Sleeping arrangements are dormitory style with bunk beds. They can accommodate up to fourteen people. You will need to bring your own bedding.  We also ask that you leave the cabins and grounds in the condition you found them. Check-in will be after 5:30pm on Friday, July 13 because of legal requirements involving the "day camp" which concludes at that time.

Many bonspiel participants will prefer hotel/motel accommodations, others will stay with friends or relatives, and a few have cottages in the area. This option is for those who are looking for a lower cost alternative to a hotel/motel room. One that offers an opportunity for more social contact with other curlers, includes a variety of things to do on-site or just a quiet place to relax during their off-ice hours.

The options for housing at Camp Alexander are (1) as an individual, (2) as a couple, (3) as a team of four, (4) a tent, (5) bring your own RV or travel trailer. Please tell us which option you are requesting and the names of those who will be joining you. Do you prefer co-ed, all female or male quarters. We will try our best to compy with all requests.

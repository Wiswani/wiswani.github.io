Bible Study

Join us Tuesdays at 10 am or 6 pm as we walk through the Bible together.  Whether you like to discuss things or just like to sit back and listen, you’re welcome to join us.  We typically use a guide book to help us with our study (and one can be ordered easily through the office) if you’d like … but it’s not necessary.  Any questions?  Feel free to contact Pastor Josh.

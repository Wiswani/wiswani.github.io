Welcome .. Love .. Grow .. Go

Each and every person is loved by God more than they can imagine. You matter to God, You matter to us-so come as you are.  If you are currently not active in a church, our faith family is here for you. We invite you to join with us in discovering God’s LOVE; to GROW in our faith in and love for God; and to GO and be disciples and serve God by serving others. We will be the first ones to admit that we’re not perfect though, but if that’s something you can relate with you’ll fit right in.

 The Wisconsin Rapids United Methodist Church congregation has had a presence in Wisconsin Rapids for over 150 years, however, we are beginning a new journey. We're looking to start afresh knowing that the way ministry is done today is much different than it was 20, 30, or 150 years ago.

We invite you on this journey with us, and to join in on something new and unfolding. We're doing our best to grow in God's grace and love, and let others know about this same grace and love that has affected our lives.

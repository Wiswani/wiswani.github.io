Majuila Falls Agricultural Centre

Mujila Falls Agriculture Centre is an official project of the General Board of Global Ministries of the United Methodist Church.
It was founded in the year 2000 with a grant of 2500 acres of land from a Lunda Chief named Kanyama. We have been developing this land as a research centre and training facility for local entry level farmers.
We focus primarily on food production and increasing the protein, vitamin and mineral levels in the local diets and increasing incomes for local people.

Imagine No Malaria

The Imagine No Malaria program aims to reduce the number of deaths and illnesses caused by malaria in African communities through comprehensive, community-based malaria programs and quality health services delivery at United Methodist Church and affiliated health facilities.

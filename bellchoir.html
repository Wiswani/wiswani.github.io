Bell Choir

Handbells provide a unique opportunity to “make a joyful noise to the Lord” and add a distinctive sparkle to worship services.

Our current bell choir traces it's roots back to the early nineteen sixties. Our bell choir consists of players from various musical backgrounds and experience. The things that we all have in common is our love of God, music, and others. We come together to praise the Lord and inspire our congregation through our music. We also enjoy having a little fun along the way. 

If interested in joining our group, the bell choir director can be contacted at fritzkronenberg@yahoo.com
